Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on your location. You can see the shipping options available to you during checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all our products. Items must be returned in their original packaging and in unused condition to be eligible for a full refund.

Are your car toys suitable for children?

Our premium car toys are primarily designed for collectors and enthusiasts. While some models may be suitable for older children, many contain small parts and are not recommended for children under 14 years of age.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please contact our sales team at wholesale@luxcars.com for more information and to discuss your requirements.
